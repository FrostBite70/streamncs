Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 19, 2026

At WASDClick, accessible from https://wasdclick.com, the privacy of our visitors is of paramount importance. This Privacy Policy document outlines the types of information that are collected and recorded by third-party services on our website and how this data is utilized.

WASDClick is operated by an individual. We do not require account registration, do not run email newsletters, do not use affiliate marketing links, and do not directly collect, store, or process any personal data from our visitors.

If you have additional questions or require more information about our Privacy Policy, please contact us directly via email at contactus@wasdclick.com.

1. Consent

By utilizing our website, you hereby consent to our Privacy Policy and agree to its terms.

2. Information Collected by Third Parties

While WASDClick does not collect any personal data directly, this website uses Google AdSense to serve advertisements. Google, as a third-party vendor, uses technology to serve ads that appear on our website.

Google DoubleClick DART Cookie

Google uses cookies, specifically DART cookies, to serve ads to our site visitors based upon their visit to https://wasdclick.com and other sites on the internet. These cookies track browsing behavior to deliver personalized advertisements.

  • Visitors may opt-out of the use of the DART cookie by visiting the Google Ad and Content Network Privacy Policy at the following URL: https://google.com

Automated Technologies

Third-party ad servers or ad networks use technologies like cookies, JavaScript, or Web Beacons within their respective advertisements and links. These technologies are sent directly to your browser and automatically record your IP address. They are used to measure the effectiveness of advertising campaigns and to personalize the advertising content you see on websites.

Please note that WASDClick has no access to or control over these cookies or technologies used by third-party advertisers.

3. Log Files

Like most standard websites, WASDClick utilizes hosting services that generate standard log files. These files log visitors when they access the website. The information inside the log files includes internet protocol (IP) addresses, browser type, Internet Service Provider (ISP), date/time stamp, referring/exit pages, and the number of clicks. This data is not linked to any information that is personally identifiable.

WASDClick's Privacy Policy does not apply to other advertisers or websites. 5. CCPA Privacy Rights

Under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), California consumers have specific rights regarding their data. Because WASDClick does not collect or sell your personal data, your data is inherently protected. However, third-party advertising partners like Google may process data. You retain the right to request disclosure, deletion, or to stop the sale of personal data.

If you wish to make a request or exercise these rights regarding third-party ad settings on our site, please contact us at contactus@wasdclick.com.

6. GDPR Data Protection Rights

We want to ensure you are fully aware of your data protection rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). Every visitor is entitled to the rights of access, rectification, erasure, processing restriction, objection, and data portability.

If you make a request, we have one month to respond to you. Please contact us at contactus@wasdclick.com to initiate any inquiries.

7. Children's Information

Protecting children using the internet is a top priority. WASDClick does not knowingly collect any Personal Identifiable Information from children under the age of 13. If you believe your child has provided personal information on our website, we strongly encourage you to contact us immediately, and we will remove such records.
